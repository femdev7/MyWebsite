Hot Dogs

Here is my unique gourmet hot dog recipe.

Ingredients

  • Hot dogs
  • Hot dog buns
  • Mustard
  • Relish
  • Chopped onions

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to 350°.
  2. Place dogs on grill, roll occasionally for even cooking.
  3. Place cooked dogs in buns.
  4. Apply mustard, relish, and onions to taste.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
